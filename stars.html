What We Stand For
1
A Strong Start Every Day
The Stars believe morning meeting sets the tone for the whole class. A president who leads it well makes every student feel like they belong and know what's coming. When the day starts with energy and clarity, everyone does better.
Our promise: Morning meeting will never be boring or skipped.
2
Every Voice Gets Heard
The Stars believe a great leader listens before they speak. When the class gets to make a decision together, a Stars president makes sure quiet voices get a chance, not just the loudest ones. Good ideas can come from anyone.
Our promise: No one gets left out of the conversation.
3
Lead by Example
The Stars believe the best leaders don't just talk about doing the right thing -- they do it first. A Stars president shows kindness, listens well, and keeps a good attitude even when things are hard. The class watches what their leader does.
Our promise: We will be the example we want to see.
The Stars Pledge
As a member of The Stars, I believe that how we start our day matters.
I will speak up, listen well, and make sure my classmates feel seen.
I will lead with energy, kindness, and purpose -- every single day.
