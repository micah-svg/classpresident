What We Stand For
1
Fair Solutions for Everyone
The Stripes believe a president's most important job is solving problems without taking sides. When classmates disagree, a Stripes president listens to both sides, stays calm, and finds a solution that feels fair -- not just for their friends, but for the whole class.
Our promise: We will always hear both sides before deciding.
2
Calm Wins Over Chaos
The Stripes believe a leader who stays calm under pressure makes better decisions. When things get messy or unfair, a Stripes president doesn't panic or pick favorites. They take a breath, think it through, and help the class get back on track with clear, steady leadership.
Our promise: We will be the calm in the storm.
3
Decisions That Belong to Everyone
The Stripes believe that when the class gets to choose something fun, the process matters as much as the result. A Stripes president runs a fair vote, makes sure every student has a real say, and accepts the outcome -- even if it's not what they personally wanted.
Our promise: The class decides together. Always.
The Stripes Pledge
As a member of The Stripes, I believe that fairness is the foundation of everything.
I will stay calm, listen carefully, and stand up for what is right -- not just what is easy.
I will lead with honesty, steadiness, and respect for every person in this room.
